About Us

Hasan Juma Backer Trading & Contracting Co LLC today is one of the Leading Civil Electro-Mechanical Construction and Project Management Companies in Oman with an international presence. This growth in three decades from a small villa sector contractor in 1975 has all the hallmarks of setting sights firmly on future and striving hard to achieve pre-eminence.

A Management firmly rooted in Quality Culture, a dedicated workforce striving for skill enhancement, seamlessly integrated management systems, constantly upgraded storage, procurement and sourcing methods, efficient supply chain management and latest methods in project programming are features that has enabled HJB to achieve this distinction of a company with a difference, a company that is willing to accept new challenges (as it did very successfully when it took up forays into roads and water sectors) and a company with social conscience.

HJB was one of the first companies in construction sector in Oman to implement and third party certify a quality management system to ISO 9002:1994 as early as Dec 2000. Today HJB is ISO 9001:2008 certified for carrying out:

“Civil, electro-mechanical engineering construction and project management, installation and commissioning for power & water transmission & distribution systems for waste water, infrastructure developments, aluminum fabrication and carpentry/joinery”

Since 1975, Hasan Juma Backer Trading & Contracting Co LLC (HJB) is providing Civil, Electro-Mechanical Construction Services to a wide spectrum of clients including Diwan of Royal Court, Ministries, Royal Oman Police and important establishments in Public and Private Sectors. The Company is also registered with PDO.

The civil electro-mechanical construction experience spectrum encompasses landmark buildings in Muscat (like HQ buildings of RGO, OmanGas, Oman LNG, State General Reserve Fund, College of Banking & Finance etc), Hospitals (Wadi Heibi, 100 bed extension to Jalan Bani Bu Ali Hospital), University Colleges and Schools (Rustaq, Ibra, Several Buildings in SQU & Many MOE Schools), major infrastructure works including Industrial Estates (Sohar, Raysut, Russayl, Sur), Roads (Yeti-Shifah, Maintenance of approx 4500 KMs of graded roads etc), Water Works (Water supply to Muscat Governorate, Busher, Barkha, Ash’Sharqiah Sands, Al Massarat, Al Khod, Ibra etc) and Waste Water Management (STPs - Nizwa, Samail, Rustaq, Masirah).

The company has gone international by taking up a major project in the Water Sector in the State of Qatar after successfully completing a techno-commercial evaluation on its own strength against International Companies.

The corporate MISSION STATEMENT is “To deliver customer expectations – every time.” and this is supported by its corporate CORE VALUES “Uncompromising commitment to Quality, Safety and Teamwork” and the KEY RESOURCES “Human Endeavor supported by adequate finance and infrastructure”

The corporate vision is “To attain and sustain market leadership position in the fields of Civil & Electro-Mechanical Construction and make positive contributions to the society through development and deployment of local resources.” This vision formally put in writing in 2000 is well on the way to realization. HJB is already a major and key player in the market and has been awarded the Green Card for meaningful contribution to the Omanisation process. Nationalizing technical expertise through induction and training of Omanis is the credo of the Group.

The dedicated, committed and motivated team of technocrats, management professionals, engineers, technicians and trained labor force with an uncompromising commitment to quality and safety help HJB deliver customer satisfaction – every time. The essence of HJB’s success is its professionalism.

Electro-Mechanical Division


It is a natural corollary for a company of HJB’s stature and developmental thrust to focus on Construction in its entirety and add capabilities to execute Electro-Mechanical projects to its formidable strength in Civil construction.

By inducting professionals HJB has been able to offer its clientele a turnkey option to their construction requirements.

HJB’s ability to marshal its resources and mobilize in remote locations has enabled this Division to execute projects in very far-flung areas and contribute its mite to the universal electrification and drinking water distribution to the Nation.
